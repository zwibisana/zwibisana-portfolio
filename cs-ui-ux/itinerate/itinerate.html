about

From May 2024 to June 2024, I went on a study abroad trip to Japan where I took two design classes, Experience Design Principles (ARTG3462) and Experience and Interaction (ARTF2222). There, I learned basic experience mapping, how to create narratives from user journeys, and interactivity through AR.

After conducting user research interviews with my peers, I deducted that one of the biggest concerns common among students in my cohort were that making plans with friends can be difficult because people may have varying interests.

For my Experience and Interaction class, I decided to design a hi-fidelity prototype of a travel app using Figma that would help people plan trips with their friends through a “blend” that resembles the feature that shows shared favorite songs between two people on Spotify!
