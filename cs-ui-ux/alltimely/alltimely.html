about

The AllTimely app is a scheduling and activity management app designed specifically for schools and educational institutions. It allows students to easily find and sign up for extracurricular activities and clubs, while also providing helpful features for club leaders and advisors to manage and communicate with their groups.

As students, we understood how AllTimely could be a powerful tool for anyone looking to get involved in their school's community, so we made it our goal to increase extracurricular engagement and make using the app quicker and simpler through direct user research.
